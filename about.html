My name is Megan Brantley, and art
has always been a huge part of my life.
I consider my talent a blessing, and I
hope to use my talent as a career one
day. Any profit I make currently goes to
funding the many adventures I want to
go on. I love to travel, but the costs can
be so expensive. By supporting me, you
are helping me see the world!

Please feel free to contact me about
commissioning artwork for you or
donating to this starving artist!
